ITIS 3135 Website Evaluations

Good Website: Apple

Apple.com is the official website of Apple Inc., a renowned technology company known for its innovative products and services. The site serves as a comprehensive online platform where users can explore the latest Apple products, access customer support, and stay updated on company news and events.

CRAP Principles Assessment for Apple.com:

  • Contrast: Effectively utilizes contrast in its design, with a clean white background and high-contrast product images and text, ensuring content is easily legible and visually appealing.
  • Repetition: Maintains a consistent layout and design throughout, employing the signature Apple aesthetic, which reinforces its brand identity and offers a seamless user experience.
  • Alignment: Aligns elements logically and symmetrically, promoting a sense of order and organization in its content.
  • Proximity: Information and interactive elements are strategically placed in close proximity to related content, aiding user navigation and comprehension.

Overall, Apple.com adheres well to the CRAP principles, with a sleek and organized design that enhances the user experience and reinforces the company's brand image.

Bad Website: Arngren

Arngren.net is a website that offers a wide range of products and items for sale, covering various categories such as electronics, gadgets, home goods, and more. The site showcases an extensive catalog of products at competitive prices, making it an online shopping destination for those seeking budget-friendly options.

CRAP Principles Assessment for Arngren.net:

  • Contrast: Struggles with contrast in its design, as it often features cluttered pages with small fonts and busy backgrounds, making it challenging for users to distinguish between different elements.
  • Repetition: Lacks consistency in its layout and design, with various font styles, colors, and formatting choices, leading to a disjointed and unprofessional appearance.
  • Alignment: Elements appear haphazardly placed, leading to a chaotic and disorganized visual presentation.
  • Proximity: Information and products are sometimes placed too closely together or too far apart, creating confusion and hindering user navigation.

Overall, Arngren.net struggles to adhere to the CRAP principles, with its cluttered and inconsistent design compromising user experience and visual appeal. Improvements in these areas could enhance the site's overall usability and professionalism.
