Wireframe and Site Map:

Page Design:

The main pages that I will be implementing are the home page, products, product details, about us, shopping cart, and checkout. The rest of the pages shown in the site map are just different versions of the same page technically but I just wanted to include it for my own sake

Home Page

  • Purpose: Introduce the business and showcase featured products.
  • Audience: Customers
  • Content: Welcome message, featured products, promotions.
  • Interactivity: Image sliders, quick view options.
  • Asking User to enter data: No
  • Contain buttons, hyperlinks, or dropdowns: Yes
  • Special Notes: Keep it visually appealing.

Products

  • Purpose: Display a variety of clothing products.
  • Audience: Customers.
  • Content: Grid of product images with brief descriptions.
  • Asking User to enter data: No
  • Contain buttons, hyperlinks, or dropdowns: Yes
  • Interactivity: Sorting and filtering options.
  • Special Notes: Ensure a clear call-to-action for each product.

Product Details

  • Purpose: Provide detailed information about a specific product.
  • Audience: Customers.
  • Content: Large product image, detailed description, price.
  • Asking User to enter data: No
  • Contain buttons, hyperlinks, or dropdowns: Yes
  • Interactivity: Add to cart button.
  • Special Notes: Include customer reviews if available.

About Us

  • Purpose: Inform customers about what the business is and how to contact them.
  • Audience: Customers.
  • Content: little description about who the client is, contact info, locations, and other business related information.
  • Asking User to enter data: No
  • Contain buttons, hyperlinks, or dropdowns: Yes
  • Interactivity: not much because more for information
  • Special Notes: Try to talk about the family business to connect with customers

Shopping Cart

  • Purpose: Summarize selected items for purchase.
  • Audience: Customers.
  • Content: List of selected items, total price.
  • Asking User to enter data: Yes
  • Data Validation: Yes
  • Contain buttons, hyperlinks, or dropdowns: Yes
  • Interactivity: Remove items, proceed to checkout.
  • Special Notes: Ensure clear navigation back to product pages.

Checkout

  • Purpose: Collect shipping details and payment information.
  • Audience: Customers.
  • Content: Form fields for shipping, payment options.
  • Asking User to enter data: Yes
  • Data Validation: Yes
  • Contain buttons, hyperlinks, or dropdowns: Yes
  • Interactivity: Form validation, confirmation message.
  • Special Notes: Provide a clear summary before finalizing the purchase.

Dynamic Functionality

  • Navigation Bar and Header/Footer: Across all pages
  • Form Validation: Ensure that user input in checkout forms is valid.
  • Interactive Images: Zoom or additional views for product images.
  • Shopping Cart Updates: Dynamically update the cart without refreshing the page.
  • AJAX for Loading Products: Use AJAX to load product listings without refreshing the entire page.
